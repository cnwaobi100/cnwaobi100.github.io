Undergraduate School

I am an undergradute mechanical engineering major from Houston, Texas. During my time as an undergraduate student at Texas A&M University, I have excelled in my classes and have learned much more than what was expected of me. I have spent each semester taking my required classes in order to graduate and have achieved a passing grade in all of them. With the help of my friends that I have met along the way, I have truly received a genuine excitement to learn and improve not only my grades, but myself. I believe that I have been blessed with one of the greatest opportunities to succeed, the chance to attend Texas A&M University and perform well in the department of Mechanical Engineering isn't something that everyone is given, which is why I am so grateful.

Community Involvement

I believe that one of the most important and rewarding feelings in life is when you are given the chance or opportunity to give back to someone or something that is in need of assistance. Giving back to the community that I have been raised in or went to school in is something that I have always found myself doing. From volunteering to tutor elementary and middle schoolers through my 4 years in high school, to volunteering to help patients at the hospital that my mom works at for an entire summer, to being a part of a leadership and motivatin organization during my sophomore and junior year at Texas A&M, volunteering and community involvement is something that is near and very dear to my heart and to my overall character.

My One Wish

Live Life One Moment At A Time

Throughout my life, I have realized that there are certain moments that occur that are truly beautiful and worth cherishing and sharing forever. There's always this question of, "If you could have any superpower in the world, which would you have and why?" and here is my answer to that question. While having the power to fly or to turn invisible may be cool, the one power I would want to have is the power to relive a moment in my life and experience that feeling once again.

But why would I "waste" an opporunity on such a wish like that? Because those moments, those once in a lifetime opportunities to experience a new smile or laugh or genuine happy feeling in your heart, are the moments that make life worth living. Those are the moments that are like none before, but they come at the cost of only experiencing them once.

The purpose of this website, portfolio, blog, whatever you want to call it, is to highlight those moments. Those moments where I cried the most, laughed the most, was angry the most, achieved the most, or was genuinely in love with life the most. Those moments will have a photo attached to them where you can click on the image and read about that specific event. It is meant to give my perspective and to hopefully spread the message to enjoy life one memory at a time and to fully immerse yourself in the moment, because it is the only chance you'll get.
